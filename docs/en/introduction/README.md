## What is Optima Prevent?

**Optima Prevent** is an advanced web-based EHS (Environmental Health and Safety) system designed for comprehensive workplace safety and preventive measures management. The application is the result of Slovenian expertise and innovation by **A1 Informatika**.

## ğŸ¯ System Purpose

Optima Prevent enables companies to:
- **Systematically track** preventive measures
- **Manage workplace risks** effectively
- **Conduct electronic testing** of employees  
- **Manage documentation** and certificates
- **Track compliance** with legislation
- **Automate routine** administrative tasks
